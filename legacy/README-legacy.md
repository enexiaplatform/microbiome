## 🚀 SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE & FEATURES

### 1. Client-Facing (Customer Landing)
- **Premium Aesthetics:** Emerald green brand, Hanken Grotesk typography, dark/light mode, smooth scroll reveal animations
- **Product Discovery Quiz:** 3-question interactive quiz routing visitors to the right EnviroBiotics system (BioLogic Mini / Biotica 800 / E Biotic Home)
- **Product Catalog:** 4 products with coverage specs, pricing, features, certifications, and affiliate links
- **Starter Kit Bundles:** 4 curated combos for higher AOV promotions
- **Knowledge Library:** 9 science-backed educational articles for SEO and audience trust

### 2. Founder Hub (Affiliate Dashboard)
- **KPIs & Campaign Log:** Track views, clicks, orders, revenue, CTR, and CR. SVG trend charts. Import/export CSV.
- **7-Day Onboarding Roadmap:** Step-by-step affiliate launch guide with progress tracking
- **Product Scorer:** 7-criteria weighted scoring model for evaluating any affiliate product
- **Video Script Hub:** 36 complete content scripts (4 products × 3 angles: problem, myth-busting, science-backed) optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts
- **Affiliate Link Config:** Centralized affiliate URL management — update once, propagates site-wide
- **Weekly Review Generator:** Auto-generates structured markdown performance reports from campaign data
- **Science Lookup:** Live search of Europe PMC / PubMed for peer-reviewed studies to back content claims

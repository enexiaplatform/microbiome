## Editorial guardrails for every script

- **No medical/health claims.** Never "kills germs," "eliminates allergens," "relieves allergies," or any health-outcome framing — attributed or not.
- **Manufacturer claims stay attributed.** Anything a brand says about its product is "the manufacturer states…" — never asserted in our editorial voice.
- **Hospital studies ≠ home results.** Every science script says the setting was hospitals/institutions and that home-use evidence is limited.
- **Nothing invented.** No personal testing, no testimonials, no "we tried it," no prices or studies beyond the site sources. The BioLogic Mini review is research-based, not hands-on — scripts say so.
- **Deliberately excluded:** PureBiotics Probiotic Pet Cleaner (price/details UNVERIFIED per `products.ts`) — not featured in any script until verified.

## Posting checklist

Before any of these scripts goes live, confirm all of the following:

1. **FTC disclosure.** If a post ever includes an affiliate link, it needs a clear, unmissable `#ad`-style disclosure in the caption itself (not buried in a hashtag cloud, not only on the linked page). Baseline: FTC Endorsement Guides, 16 CFR Part 255. When in doubt, disclose.
2. **Link routing.** Link-in-bio points to the site article, /quiz, or /reset-guide on themicrobiomehome.com — never directly to an affiliate or retailer URL. On-page affiliate disclosure renders before the first affiliate link on the destination article; verify it before driving traffic.
3. **Amazon links never in DMs or email.** Amazon Associates links may not be shared via direct message, email, or any offline/private channel. If a follower DMs asking for a link, reply with the site article URL only.
4. **Claims re-check.** Re-read the script against CLAIMS_POLICY.md before posting: no health-outcome claims, every product claim phrased "the manufacturer states…", hospital findings framed with the home-use caveat, no banned phrases ("kills 99.9%", "eliminates allergens", "vet recommended", etc.).
5. **No invented specifics.** Prices, study figures, and product specs in the script match `src/data/products.ts` and the source article exactly. Do not ad-lib refill prices (Biotica 800 refill pricing is UNVERIFIED), discounts, certifications, or results.
6. **UNVERIFIED items stay out.** The PureBiotics Probiotic Pet Cleaner and any unverified affiliate-program terms are not mentioned in organic or paid social until verified in `products.ts`.
7. **No medical advice in comments.** Replies about pet health, allergies, or symptoms get the standard pointer: consult your veterinarian (pets) or your doctor (people). Never recommend a product "for" a health condition.
8. **Music/licensing.** Use only platform-licensed audio; the spoken hook must remain intelligible under any track.
9. **Accessibility.** Burned-in captions on every post (spoken scripts above are the caption source text, verbatim).
10. **Quarterly audit.** These scripts are re-checked against CLAIMS_POLICY.md and current manufacturer claims at each quarterly content audit (Jan / Apr / Jul / Oct); stale attributions are corrected or the post is removed.
